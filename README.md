# 🍕 Pizza Sales Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Pizza Sales Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence solution built in **Power BI** to analyze pizza sales performance, customer ordering behavior, and product trends.

This dashboard enables business stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify best- and worst-selling pizzas, understand customer purchasing patterns, and make data-driven decisions to improve sales and profitability.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance
- Monitor customer ordering trends
- Identify top and bottom-selling pizzas
- Understand sales distribution by category and size
- Discover peak sales days and hours
- Support inventory and marketing decisions

---

## 💡 Business Insights

- Peak sales occur during weekends and evening hours.
- Large-sized pizzas contribute the highest revenue.
- Classic category generates the highest sales.
- A small number of pizzas contribute significantly to total revenue.
- Several low-performing pizzas may require menu optimization.
- Customer ordering behavior shows clear seasonal and weekly trends.
